Description

Babies & Basilisks is an asymmetric VR party game. One player, wearing a VR headset, builds a level from prebuilt sections. Up to four other players can choose between four unique characters and navigate through the level in a third-person action game. The goal of these players is to reach a specific end goal, while VR player tries to stop them using enemies, traps, and a boss, which the VR player can directly control.
